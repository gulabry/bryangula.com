What exactly is CardSwapr?

CardSwapr is the first EVER User-to-User trading and buying application for Gift Cards. Every other gift card company makes you mail in your old cards to get credit or cash. With CardSwapr, never again will you have to mail in an old gift cards to recieve value for your cards. We easily distribute electronic Gift Cards to mobile phones for anytime use. NO more carrying plastic cards. NO more drawers of unused or unwanted cards. With our app, all you have to do is take a picture of your unwanted gift card then the funds are instantly added to your account to use in any of three ways: click the next tab to see how!

Sell

If you want to quickly get rid of an Old Navy gift card you don’t want, you can quickly sell that card to us for a credit to your account. This credit can be used for any purchase in our system (Other retailer cards, cards users are selling, or purchases at stores).

Swap

This feature is best for friends who want to swap cards with one another. All they have to do is agree to a swap and they can exchange cards of any value for free.

Trade

This is the hallmark feature of CardSwap. For the first time, you can trade gift cards between a vast network of users on your phone. This has never before been utilized on a mobile device. A card in your wallet can be visible to other users who can buy your unwanted gift cards. Then, that card will immediately be added to the buyer’s personal wallet.
