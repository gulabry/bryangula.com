About Me:

Over the past several years I've been very passionate about interaction, UX/UI, and web design. I also have extensive experience in leadership through my time in theatre, my position as a Resident Advisor, and my new role as the CTO for my mobile app, CardSwapr. As I pursue my BS in Informatics (with a focus on Human-Computer Interaction) at the University of Washington, my growing portfolio of projects and unmatched work ethic will lead to an exciting and eventful career in these fields.
